Rise of Draksner

A demo preview of the RPG game Rise of Draksner entirely made in RPGMaker

Information

  • Title: Rise of Draksner
  • Genere: RPG, Adventure, Action
  • Platform: PC/Windows
  • Lenguage: Catalan

Responsabilities

Alongside my university colleagues, we were tasked with creating and developing a world and its narrative. We created Rise of Draksner. We were able to document all that was needed: A map of the island and its regions, character design, game balancing, dialog scripts and storytelling. Finally, in order to consolidate the project and to be able to put a face on the game and see if it was working properly or not, we developed it on RPG Maker V. With this, we were able to play our game fast with a decent end!
