Legend of Landama

A JRPG game developed for the Project II subject using SDL2

Information

  • Title: Legend of Landama
  • Genere: RPG, Adventure, Action
  • Platform: PC/Windows
  • Lenguage: English

Responsabilities

Alongside with my university colleagues, we were tasked with developing an RPG videogame for the subject Project II of our Videogame Design & Development Degree. We came up with the idea of an Isekai themed narrative along with a classic RPG battle system. All entirely made with SDL2 Library The result is this demo game, available for download right now!
