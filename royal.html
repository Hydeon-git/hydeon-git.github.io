Dreams of Royalty

Dreams of Royalty is a medieval narrative experience build into a video game.

Information

  • Title: Dreams of Royalty
  • Genre: Adventure
  • Platform: PC/Windows
  • Language: Spanish
  • Engine: Unity

Responsibilities

As a group of 4 people, I was responsible to design some of the key mechanics that will create the narrative experience of the project.
I was responsible of designing the level where the game takes place, in this case, a castle. Using a modular asset we accomplished the overall structure of the castle, having various corridors and an outdoors area with trees and a lake.
My last contribution in the development was to program basic features like character movement, cleaning system and the implementation of a little cinematic.
