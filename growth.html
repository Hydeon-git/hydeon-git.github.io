Growth

A platformer game developed in Unity for our superior grade final project.

Information

  • Title: Growth
  • Genere: Platformer, Fantasy
  • Platform: PC/Windows
  • Lenguage: English

Responsabilities

Growth was one of the most ambitious game projects I've been. It's the creation of the idea we had for our final project in superior grade. As a group of 4 people, my main task was to program most of the basic gameplay of the game and its mechanics. I designed the initial structure of the level implemented which later was polished along with a colleague. We wanted that the player could feel the emotions from the scenario, and using colors, environment asset changes and interactive music, we created Growth! Synopsis: Growth revolves around our protagonist who lies in the fact that he is weak and unable to defend himself after he loses all his powers after a certain incident. He is trapped in a cave now. It seems that he will have to learn again his skills if he wants to make it out and know the truth about what really happened.
