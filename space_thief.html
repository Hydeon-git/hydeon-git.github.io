Space Thief

A little platformer game developed in SDL2 for the subject Game Development on university

Information

  • Title: Space Thief
  • Genere: Platformer, Sci-fi, Pixelart
  • Platform: PC/Windows
  • Lenguage: English

Responsabilities

For our subject about Game Development, we programed in pairs, a simple platformer game: Space Thief. In this subject, we learned more advanced programming skills like pathfinding, collision setup, gravity, entities, and scene changes. The result is this short platformer game ambiented on a futuristic spaceship, where or protagonist arrives to steal its treasures.
